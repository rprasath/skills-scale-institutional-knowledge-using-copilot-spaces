## Core Roles

The following roles are essential to OctoAcme projects. For detailed descriptions, see [OctoAcme Personas](./octoacme-roles-and-personas.md).

**Leadership & Strategy:**
- **Product Manager (PdM)**: Defines outcomes, prioritizes backlog, measures success
- **Project Manager (PM)**: Coordinates delivery, manages schedules, risks, and communications
- **Business Analyst**: Gathers and documents business requirements; translates them into actionable specs

**Delivery & Quality:**
- **Developers**: Implement features, collaborate on design and testability
- **UX Designer**: Creates user-centered experiences and design specifications
- **QA/Testing**: Validates quality and acceptance criteria

**Process & Operations:**
- **Scrum Master**: Facilitates Agile ceremonies, removes impediments, enables team self-organization
- **Release Manager**: Plans, coordinates, and communicates releases to minimize risk

**Governance:**
- **Stakeholders**: Provide inputs, approvals, and sponsorship

For collaboration patterns and decision authority, see the [Role Collaboration Matrix](./octoacme-role-collaboration-matrix.md).
